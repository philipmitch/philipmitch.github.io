About Me

I am a student at George Brown College looking to work as a freelance UX Designer upon graduating from the Interaction Design program. I am looking to help clients rebrand their business or build a successful start-up business by using some of the best and most recent design practices.

I believe an effective brand identity will visually communicate the personality and values of the business. It will allow the customers to see what the brand is about, what it stands for and how they can expect to feel when they interact with it.

"I help startups and digital agencies to design intuitive websites and apps people love to use".

services

Branding

Branding helps create a strong and impactful identity by using consistent span styling throughout your company.

Design

Photography

Capturing special moments and creating content for your various platforms helps your audience develop a personal connection with you brand.

Research

User research focuses on understanding user behaviours, needs, and motivations through observation techniques, task analysis, and other feedback methodologies.

Strategy

Strategy is all about trying to merge the business objective with creative solutions through planning and design thinking to ensure you brand has the best possible roadmap for success.
